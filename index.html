About Aurum Dental Studio

Where Mumbai Comes for Healthy, Beautiful Smiles

Tucked into the heart of Bandra West, Aurum Dental Studio is the culmination of Dr. Aanya Mehta's vision — a practice where clinical excellence meets genuine warmth. For over two decades, we've cared for Mumbai families, Bollywood smiles, and everyone in between.

Our studio is equipped with German-engineered dental technology, sterilised to international standards, and designed to feel less like a clinic and more like a calm retreat. Every treatment plan is built around you — your comfort, your goals, your budget.
